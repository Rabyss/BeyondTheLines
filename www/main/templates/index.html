Number of Words per Sentence

The number of words per sentence can provide a measure of the complexity of a text. For example, the masterpiece In Search of Lost Time by French writer Marcel Proust has an average of 38 words per sentence, while that's of a children can be lower than 10.

Mood

The mood of a text is computed from the use of modality verbs and adverbs. It can be indicative, imperative, conditional or subjunctive.

Modality

The modality of a sentence represents its degree of certainty. For example, the ssentence "I wish it would stop raining" has a rather low modality, whereas "It will stop raining" has a high one. Accuracy is about 68% for Wikipedia texts.
